Groupdot began it’s journey under the prominent leadership of Mr. Al Rashed Prodhan, Mrs. Syeda Nafisa Reza and Mr. Masud Rana. Mrs. Syeda Nafisa reza plays the role of honorable chairperson. Groupdot comprises of 4 main concerns. It has diversified its ventures in several areas, especially in the Marketing sector.

Groupdot officially started in April 2014 previously under the name of Dotcreat 360 Marketing. Later it became a private limited company, Dotcreat Ltd, an advertising agency which provides various creative services to the reputed companies of this country.

In January 2017, Groupdot started a Digital Agency under the name “Digidot Ltd”. It has a dynamic squad to solve any digital problem in a SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Realistic and Time Bound) way.

In August 2017, Groupdot has launched another concern of interior and architect firm under the name “Dot Arch Ltd”. It has an innovative team who dream big and find the creative solution to the required problem.

In December 2017, another concern was launched under the name “DotFrame Ltd” which is a photography & videography concern focusing on Photography of fashion, food, events, wedding, corporate program, nature and wildlife etc.

Today, Groupdot comprises of four successful companies to serve in different categories.
